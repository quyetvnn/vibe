Vibe Centro, the latest residential project is located at Kai Tak

Development Area in Kowloon City District. The developer is Poly

Property Group Co., Limited. The project name “Lung Yu” in Cantonese

pronunciation; "Lung" implies the core area of ​​the core Kai Tak district in Kowloon; “Yu” refers to the reputation of the Group.

In order to provide better services to distinguished residents, Vibe

Centro designed a Vibe Centro residents mobile app. Residents can

receive the latest information of the estate, such as estate notice or

event organized by the estate anywhere at any time. In addition,

residents can reserve clubhouse facilities and maintenance services via the mobile app.

Residents can receive all information issued by the property

management anytime and anywhere through the mobile app.

Reservation management can be handled easily

Residents can check reservation status any time via the mobile app,

allowing them to make reservations for clubhouse facilities as well as

maintenance services without the need for residents to go to the clubhouse in person.
